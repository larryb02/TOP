How to make spaghetti!

Ingredients:

  • 12 ounces dried spaghetti
  • 4 quarts water
  • 1 tablespoon salt

  1. In a 6-quart pot, bring 4 to 6 quarts water and 1 to 1 1/2 tablespoons salt to a boil.
  2. Add 12-ounces dry spaghetti to the pot, stir to separate noodles and prevent sticking and bring water back to a boil.
  3. Cook spaghetti uncovered according to package directions; start checking for doneness 2 minutes before the suggested al dente time.
  4. Drain spaghetti, reserving up to 1 cup of cooking water to add to sauce.
  5. Add the cooked spaghetti to the sauce with a splash of the cooking water and stir to coat, thinning it out with more cooking water as necessary.
